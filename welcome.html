Welcome to Blank

Blank gives you a moment to pause before visiting distracting sites. When you navigate to a protected URL, you'll type a few mindful words before continuing.

A small ritual of intention, instead of autopilot.

    A few tips

    Pin Blank to your toolbar for quick access to settings.
    Click the extension icon anytime to open settings.
    Press Cmd+, on the pause screen to jump to settings.

    You're all set
